# DIY Arduino Battery Capacity Tester V2.0

A simple and reliable open source device designed to measure the **true capacity of rechargeable batteries** by discharging them at a constant current and calculating the delivered capacity.

The tester supports common rechargeable batteries such as **Li-ion (18650), Li-Po, NiMH, NiCd, AA, and AAA**, making it useful for battery pack building, battery recycling, and identifying low quality or counterfeit cells.

## Supported Battery Types

• Lithium-ion batteries (18650, 21700, etc.)  
• Lithium-polymer (Li-Po) batteries  
• Nickel-Metal Hydride (NiMH) batteries  
• Nickel-Cadmium (NiCd) batteries  
• AA and AAA rechargeable batteries  

---

## Applications

• Testing reclaimed 18650 batteries  
• Battery pack building  
• Battery health evaluation  
• Identifying fake or low capacity batteries  
• Educational and DIY electronics projects
